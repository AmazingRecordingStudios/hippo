Discover short phrases from classic Greek authors and hear them spoken in Ancient Greek. You don't find such phrases among the Romans.

We are looking for more people to record their voices while reading Greek (current audio files are taken from librivox). Please get in touch to join and contribute.
This is an alpha version.

More info here: https://github.com/AmazingRecordingStudios/hippo
